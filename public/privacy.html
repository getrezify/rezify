Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Rezify:

  • Email address and password (for account creation)
  • Workspace name and WhatsApp number (for notifications)
  • Reservation data you enter (guest names, dates, prices)
  • iCal URLs for Airbnb and Booking.com sync

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Service
  • To send WhatsApp notifications about your reservations
  • To sync your calendars with Airbnb and Booking.com
  • To process payments through Paddle

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL) servers. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Twilio — WhatsApp notifications
  • Paddle — payment processing
  • Vercel — hosting

5. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing hello@getrezify.com.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at hello@getrezify.com.
